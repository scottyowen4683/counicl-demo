Resource Recovery Centres

Brisbane has four Resource Recovery Centres: Chandler, Ferny Grove, Nudgee, and Willawong. Open daily 6:30 am–5:45 pm, including public holidays. Households receive 10 free waste vouchers per year. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Animals & Pets

Dog registration is mandatory. A non-desexed dog costs $157 per year. Registration can be completed online in a few minutes. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Barking dog complaints require the address and typical times. I can take your details and lodge a complaint and provide a reference number. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Rates & Payments

Roads & Maintenance

Potholes can be reported with the street name or nearest intersection. I will create a job and provide a reference number. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Streetlight faults can be reported using the pole number or nearest address. I will lodge a service request. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Overgrown verge or fallen tree issues require the exact location and a brief description. I can log it for assessment and prioritisation. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Building & Development

Garages under 10 m² and under 2.4 m high usually do not need approval. Larger structures require building approval. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Fences under 2 m height usually do not require approval. Higher fences or corner blocks may require approval. Would you like me to send you the checklist link for that?

To check zoning, setbacks, and overlays, provide your address and I will direct you to the mapping tools or request a duty planner callback. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Governance & Contact

Messages for councillors are taken and forwarded to the correct ward office based on your address. I will collect your name, suburb, issue summary, and best contact. No live transfer. Would you like me to send you the contact page link for that?

General council meetings are usually held on Tuesdays at Brisbane Square. Agendas and minutes are published online. Would you like me to send you the link for that?

Complaints or compliments can be lodged online. I will provide a reference number after submission. Would you like me to send you the link for that?
